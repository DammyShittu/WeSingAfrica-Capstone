WeSingAfrica Gospel Music Concert 2021

WeSingAfrica is an all-night gospel concert hosted by Adedamola Shittu, a lover of music and son of the Most High. Birthed by a God-inspired vision, it provides a platform where all can raise their hearts, hands and voices together as one in unfettered praise to the Almighty irrespective of tribe, denomination, creed or language. This year promises to be a life changing encounter.
